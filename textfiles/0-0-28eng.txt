Hwangdoh Jungseo Habdoh: A celestial map completed in the late Joseon Dynasty. Under the title, Seo Jojun wrote an explanatory text in red letters. The dots represent stars centered around the North Pole, and the lines connect stars to indicate constellations, with the names of the constellations written next to them. Additionally, the *equator and **celestial equator are depicted as large circles. This celestial map is kept at Sungshin Women's University Museum. 
*Equator: The orbit of the sun in the celestial sphere, inclined at 23.5 degrees to the celestial equator.
