Transparent Body: The structure that makes up our body consists of various organs such as the digestive system, circulatory system, respiratory system, and nervous system. Our body is like a factory made up of tens of thousands of components, and in order to sustain life and function, the functions and roles of each organ are crucial. Therefore, let us understand where each organ is located, as well as its function and role, through transparent body and visual screens, in order to recognize the importance of our body.
