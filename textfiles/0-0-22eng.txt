Sajeon jang chong tong: Length: 42cm, Diameter: 3cm, Weight: 1.58kg. Period of use: Joseon Dynasty. It is a long barrel leader that launches four arrows and is twice as long as aSajeon-jang-chong-tong. It was used to shoot multiple arrows at once, including cha se jeon, cha se jang jeon, and cha so jeon. This dictionary barrel leader is exhibited and stored in Kyunghee University Museum.
