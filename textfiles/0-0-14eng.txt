Wangu: Length: 61.0cm, Diameter: 28.5cm, Weight: 62.4kg. Era: Joseon Dynasty. This wangu had a shape similar to a kettle, hence it was called a wangu and also known as daenggu. Depending on the diameter, there were various types of wangu such as big-sized wangu, medium-sized wangu, small-sized wangu, etc. It was used to launch non-lethal projectiles like bi gyeok jin cheon ray which are basically firecrackers, stones, and iron rings.
