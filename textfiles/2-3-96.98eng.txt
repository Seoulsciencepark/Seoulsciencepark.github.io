Europa II: Developed by the European Launcher Development Organisation in 1971. This rocket was the second development by the European Launcher Development Organisation, which was established by seven European countries in 1964. It was launched in 1971 with the purpose of launching a communication satellite, but it exploded after approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds of flight. This failure led to the dissolution of the European Launcher Development Organisation. Length: 31.0m, Width: 3.1m, Scale: 1/10.
