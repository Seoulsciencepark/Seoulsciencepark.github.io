V-2, developed by German scientists in 1942, was a rocket with a flying range of 190km and a maximum altitude of 85km. The German military used it to bomb London during World War II. After the war, German scientists who crossed over to the United States successfully launched V-2 rockets to an altitude of 183km. Dimensions: Length: 14.0m, Width: 1.7m, Scale: 1/10.
