Hwang ja chong tong. Dimensions: 50.4cm in length, 4.0cm in diameter. It was used during both land and naval battles in the Joseon Dynasty. It was equipped with *chajungjeon or **cheolhwan for launching. This Hwangjachongtong is preserved and exhibited at the Hyunchoongsa Temple as Treasure No. 886. *Chajungjeon: medium-sized projectiles. **Cheolhwan: round-shaped iron projectiles.
