Jang Jeong 1 (CSL-1): Developed in China in 1970. As a three-stage liquid-fuel rocket, the launched artificial satellite had a weight of 173kg, a perigee of 439km, and an apogee of 2,384km, following an elliptical orbit completing one revolution around the Earth in 114 minutes. In 1984, it was successfully upgraded to Jang Jeong 3, which was capable of placing communication satellites in geostationary orbit. Length: 28.4m, Width: 2.4m, Scale: 1/10.
