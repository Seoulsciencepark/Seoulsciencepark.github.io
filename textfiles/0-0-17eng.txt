Blanggi: Length: 78.2cm, Diameter: 2.6cm, Weight: 38kg, Usage Period: Joseon Dynasty. It is a developed firearm called "Hwaseungpo", which was widely used during the Imjin War, with a *horizontal firing method, and **multiple-four ignition (structure) that allows for continuous shooting with 5 to 9 ***japo (barrels). This blanggi is displayed in the Army Museum. *Hwaseungpo: A cannon that shoots by igniting gunpowder. **Mopo: A large container for loading bullets, with one per blanggi. ***Moon: A unit for counting the number of cannons. ****Japo: A small container for loading bullets, with five per blanggi.
