Imitative Thunder Cannon:
-diameter: 21.0cm
-weight: 4kg
-era of use: Late Joseon Dynasty. Developed by Imjinwaeran, a military commander, during the Imjin War, this cannonball contained gunpowder and fragments inside, which were tightly sealed and ignited to be launched as projectiles. The length of the fuse allowed for time control. This Imitative Thunder Cannon is preserved and exhibited in the Army Museum of Korea as Treasure No. 860.
