Three-chamber gun: Length: 13.9cm, Diameter: 0.9cm, Weight: 0.13kg, Era of Use: Joseon Dynasty. It was developed during the reign of King Sejong as the smallest and lightest firearm. After loading one bullet for a long-range battle, it was used by gripping the handle with tongs. This three-chamber gun is currently preserved and exhibited at the Army Museum as treasure No. 854.
