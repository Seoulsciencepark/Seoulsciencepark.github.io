Artillery Cannon: Length: 73.0cm, Diameter: 6.3cm, Weight: 24.9kg | Era: Joseon Dynasty. It is divided into two types: a muzzle-loading cannon and a medicine cannon, and when the fuse is ignited into the gunpowder inside the medicine cannon, the gunpowder in the muzzle is ignited and fired out. The ammunition used was round shots and chain shots. This artillery cannon is exhibited and preserved in the Navy Officer Academy Museum.
