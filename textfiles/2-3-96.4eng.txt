Congreve Rocket: In the 19th century, Congreve of England developed a rocket weighing 27kg, capable of flying up to about 3km. It was used as a weapon in the Battle of Waterloo in 1812 and remained in use in Britain and Europe until the mid-19th century. Length: 0.55m, Width: 0.11m, Magnification Ratio: 2.6 times.
