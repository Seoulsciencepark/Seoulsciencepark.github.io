Titan 34D: Developed in the United States in 1982, the Titan series projectile is designed to place satellites and other cargo into geosynchronous, medium Earth, or low Earth orbits. It can deliver 11.99 tons to geosynchronous orbit and 125 tons to low Earth orbit. It was launched four times until 1985. Length: 45.77 meters, width: 44.00 meters, scale: 1/10.
